Gluten and Dairy free Scalloped Potatoes

 

Ingredients

  • 6c Potatoes thinly sliced
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 3/4 cups gluten-free chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • paprika

Instructions

  1. In a greased 9x11 baking dish, layer potatoes and onion.
  2. In a saucepan over medium heat, heat the oil, stir in the cornstarch until smooth. Gradually add broth, mayonnaise, salt and pepper; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thick and bubbly. Pour over potatoes. Sprinkle with paprika.
  3. Cover and bake at 325 degrees F for 2 hours or until tender.

Scalloped Potatoes (original recipe)

                                                                                                                        Bake at 375°

 

6c Potatoes, thinly sliced

 

Sauce:

6 T butter

¼ c chopped onion

¼ c flour

2 c skim milk

½ t salt

¼ t pepper

¼ t garlic powder

1 c shredded cheddar cheese

½ c shredded cheddar cheese  topping

 

In a sprayed 9x13 pan, layer half the potato slices.

 

In a sauce pan, melt butter. Sauté onions in melted butter. Whisk in flour and then milk.  Once sauce is smooth, mix in 1 c shredded cheese.

 

Pour half of cheese sauce over potato slices

* may add cubed ham over this layer *

Layer remainder of potato slices, then remainder of sauce.

* add cubed ham as desired *

Top with ½ c shredded cheese

 

Bake 45  60 minutes until fork tender

 

Enjoy!

 

 

Bread Stuffing

                                                                                                            Bake at 350° 30 min

4 c Dry bread cubes

3 T Chopped onion, if desired

1 t  Salt (1/2 t if real butter is used)

¼ t Pepper

¼ t Poultry seasoning

      Sage to taste

1/3 c melted butter or margarine

      Hot broth or water to moisten

 

Combine bread, onion, and seasonings.  If you like, cook onion till tender in a little of the butter; then combine.  Add butter and enough liquid to moisten.  Toss gently to mix.  Makes 4c of stuffing, enough for a 4 to 5 pound chicken or capon. Double the recipe for a 10lb turkey.  Quadruple will nicely fill 9x13 baking pan.

 

 

Apple Cobbler

 

Combine:     1 c. sugar

                   2 T. flour

                   ½ t. cinnamon

                   ¼ t. nutmeg

 

Toss together with 6 cups of sliced, pared tart apples.  Cook and stir over medium heat until almost tender (about 7 minutes).  Place in a greased baking dish.  Add biscuit topper.  Bake at 400 degrees about 20 minutes.  Serves 6.

 

Biscuit Topper

 

Sift together:          1 c. flour

                   1 T. sugar

                   1 ½ t. baking powder

                   ¼ t. salt

 

Cut in ¼ c. butter until it forms coarse crumbs.  Mix ¼ c. milk and 1 slightly beaten egg; add all at once to dry ingredients; stirring just until moistened.  Drop by spoonfuls over hot fruit.  (We make a double batch of the topper).
