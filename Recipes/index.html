Scalloped Potatoes

                                                                                                                        Bake at 375°

 

6c Potatoes, thinly sliced

 

Sauce:

6 T butter

¼ c chopped onion

¼ c flour

2 c skim milk

½ t salt

¼ t pepper

¼ t garlic powder

1 c shredded cheddar cheese

½ c shredded cheddar cheese – topping

 

In a sprayed 9x13 pan, layer half the potato slices.

 

In a sauce pan, melt butter. Sauté onions in melted butter. Whisk in flour and then milk.  Once sauce is smooth, mix in 1 c shredded cheese.

 

Pour half of cheese sauce over potato slices

* may add cubed ham over this layer *

Layer remainder of potato slices, then remainder of sauce.

* add cubed ham as desired *

Top with ½ c shredded cheese

 

Bake 45 – 60 minutes until fork tender

 

Enjoy!

 

 

Apple Cobbler

 

Combine:     1 c. sugar

                   2 T. flour

                   ½ t. cinnamon

                   ¼ t. nutmeg

 

Toss together with 6 cups of sliced, pared tart apples.  Cook and stir over medium heat until almost tender (about 7 minutes).  Place in a greased baking dish.  Add biscuit topper.  Bake at 400 degrees about 20 minutes.  Serves 6.

 

Biscuit Topper

 

Sift together:          1 c. flour

                   1 T. sugar

                   1 ½ t. baking powder

                   ¼ t. salt

 

Cut in ¼ c. butter until it forms coarse crumbs.  Mix ¼ c. milk and 1 slightly beaten egg; add all at once to dry ingredients; stirring just until moistened.  Drop by spoonfuls over hot fruit.  (We make a double batch of the topper).
